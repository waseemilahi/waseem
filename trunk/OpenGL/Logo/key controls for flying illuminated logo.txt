
Right Arrow and Left Arrow :  Yaw

Down Arrow and Up Arrow :   Pitch

W and Q Keys :      Roll

S                 for  :    Smooth Shading

Z                 for  :    Flat Shading

+ and -    to increment and decrement the "spin speed"      (minimum speed =0)

A         to animate or stop the animation.

F   and   B            for Forward and Backward

D  and   U             for Down and Up

L and      R            to slide camera left and right


Escape for exiting



(either lower or upper case can be used for above controls)
